## <a name="spatial-coordinate-systems"></a><span data-ttu-id="42d4c-189">空间坐标系统</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42d4c-189">Spatial coordinate systems</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="42d4c-190">所有3D 图形应用程序都使用[笛卡尔坐标系统](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/uwp/graphics-concepts/coordinate-systems)来表示它们所呈现的虚拟世界中的对象的位置和方向。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42d4c-190">All 3D graphics applications use [Cartesian coordinate systems](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/uwp/graphics-concepts/coordinate-systems) to reason about the positions and orientations of objects in the virtual worlds they render.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="42d4c-191">此类坐标系统建立了三个垂直轴, 沿该轴放置对象: X、Y 和 Z 轴。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42d4c-191">Such coordinate systems establish 3 perpendicular axes along which to position objects: an X, Y, and Z axis.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="42d4c-192">在[混合现实](mixed-reality.md)中, 你的应用程序将考虑虚拟和物理坐标系的原因。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42d4c-192">In [mixed reality](mixed-reality.md), your apps will reason about both virtual and physical coordinate systems.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="42d4c-193">Windows 调用在物理环境中具有实际意义的坐标系统, 即**空间坐标系统**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42d4c-193">Windows calls a coordinate system that has real meaning in the physical world a **spatial coordinate system**.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="42d4c-194">空间坐标系统以米为单位表示坐标值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42d4c-194">Spatial coordinate systems express their coordinate values in meters.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="42d4c-195">这意味着, 当对象在混合现实中呈现时, X、Y 或 Z 轴上相隔2个单位的每个对象将彼此分开显示2米。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42d4c-195">This means that objects placed 2 units apart in either the X, Y or Z axis will appear 2 meters apart from one another when rendered in mixed reality.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="42d4c-196">这使你可以轻松地在真实规模上呈现对象和环境。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42d4c-196">This lets you easily render objects and environments at real-world scale.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="42d4c-197">通常, 笛卡尔坐标系统可以是右手或左手。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42d4c-197">In general, Cartesian coordinate systems can be either right-handed or left-handed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="42d4c-198">Windows 上的空间坐标系统始终是右手的, 这意味着正 X 轴向右, Y 轴上点向上 (与重力对齐), 正 Z 轴指向你。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="42d4c-198">Spatial coordinate systems on Windows are always right-handed, which means that the positive X-axis points right, the positive Y-axis points up (aligned to gravity) and the positive Z-axis points towards you.</span></span>
