## <a name="spatial-coordinate-systems"></a><span data-ttu-id="60719-188">空间坐标系统</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60719-188">Spatial coordinate systems</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="60719-189">所有的 3D 图形应用程序使用[笛卡尔坐标系统](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/uwp/graphics-concepts/coordinate-systems)推断的位置和方向的呈现虚拟世界中的对象。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60719-189">All 3D graphics applications use [Cartesian coordinate systems](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/uwp/graphics-concepts/coordinate-systems) to reason about the positions and orientations of objects in the virtual worlds they render.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="60719-190">此类坐标系统建立沿其执行定位对象的 3 个垂直轴： X、 Y 和 Z 轴。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60719-190">Such coordinate systems establish 3 perpendicular axes along which to position objects: an X, Y, and Z axis.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="60719-191">在中[混合现实](mixed-reality.md)，您的应用程序将推断虚拟和物理坐标系统。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60719-191">In [mixed reality](mixed-reality.md), your apps will reason about both virtual and physical coordinate systems.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="60719-192">Windows 调用现实生活中有实际意义的坐标系**空间坐标系统**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60719-192">Windows calls a coordinate system that has real meaning in the physical world a **spatial coordinate system**.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="60719-193">坐标系统的空间来表示它们以米为单位的坐标值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60719-193">Spatial coordinate systems express their coordinate values in meters.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="60719-194">这意味着对象放置 2 个单位相隔在任一 X，Y 或 Z 轴将显示除另一个混合现实中呈现时 2 米。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60719-194">This means that objects placed 2 units apart in either the X, Y or Z axis will appear 2 meters apart from one another when rendered in mixed reality.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="60719-195">这样可以轻松地呈现对象和实际规模的环境。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60719-195">This lets you easily render objects and environments at real-world scale.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="60719-196">一般情况下，惯用右手或惯用左手，可以是笛卡尔坐标系统。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60719-196">In general, Cartesian coordinate systems can be either right-handed or left-handed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="60719-197">在 Windows 上的空间坐标系统都右手，这意味着正 x 轴向右、 向上指正 y 轴 （对齐到重力） 和正 z 轴指向面向使用者。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60719-197">Spatial coordinate systems on Windows are always right-handed, which means that the positive X-axis points right, the positive Y-axis points up (aligned to gravity) and the positive Z-axis points towards you.</span></span>
