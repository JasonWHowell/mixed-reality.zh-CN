## <a name="the-tech"></a><span data-ttu-id="f9237-107">技术人员</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-107">The tech</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-110">使用空间声音, 我们可以提供音频提示来指示用户应关闭的方向, 或让用户知道在其空间内有更多的全息影像。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-110">Spatial sound allows us to give audio cues to indicate a direction in which the user should turn, or to let the user know there are more holograms for them to see within their space.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-111">我们还可以将声音直接附加到全息图, 并持续更新布局的方向和距离, 使其看起来像是从该对象直接发出的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-111">We can also attach a sound directly to a hologram and continually update the direction and distance the hologram is from a user to make it seem as if the sound is coming directly from that object.</span></span>

## <a name="behind-the-scenes"></a><span data-ttu-id="f9237-113">幕后</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-113">Behind the scenes</span></span>

### <a name="capturing-the-audio"></a><span data-ttu-id="f9237-116">捕获音频</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-116">Capturing the audio</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-117">在我们[关于捕获 HoloTour 视觉内容的案例研究](case-study-capturing-and-creating-content-for-holotour.md)中, 我们谈论了照相机远程测试的自定义设计。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-117">In our [case study about capturing the visual content for HoloTour](case-study-capturing-and-creating-content-for-holotour.md), we talked about the custom design of our camera rig.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-118">它包含在三维打印架中包含的14个 GoPro 摄像机, 适用于特定尺寸的架。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-118">It consisted of 14 GoPro cameras contained in a 3D-printed housing, designed to the specific dimensions of the tripod.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-119">若要从该远程测试机组捕获音频, 请在相机下添加一个四重麦克风阵列, 这些阵列送入了一个紧凑型四通道的录音单元中, 该单位是一颗</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-119">To capture audio from this rig, we added a quad-microphone array beneath the cameras, which fed into a compact 4-channel recording unit that sat at the base of the tripod.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-120">我们选择的麦克风不仅表现良好, 而且占用的空间非常小, 因此无法遮蔽照相机的视图。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-120">We chose microphones that not only performed well, but which had a very small footprint, so as to not occlude the view of the camera.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-121">![自定义相机和麦克风装置](images/camera-rig-microphones-300px.png)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-121">![Custom camera and microphone rig](images/camera-rig-microphones-300px.png)</span></span><br>
<span data-ttu-id="f9237-122">*自定义相机和麦克风装置*</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-122">*Custom camera and microphone rig*</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-123">此设置从照相机的确切位置以四个方向捕获声音, 为我们提供了足够的信息来重新创建使用空间声音的3D 听觉全景, 稍后我们可以同步到360的视频。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-123">This setup captured sound in four directions from the precise location of our camera, giving us enough information to re-create a 3D aural panorama using spatial sound, which we could later synchronize to the 360-degree video.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-124">照相机阵列音频面临的难题之一是, 在捕获时所记录的受到。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-124">One of the challenges with the camera array audio is that you are at the mercy of what is recorded at the time of capture.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-125">即使视频捕获非常好, 声音捕获仍会出现问题, 因为相机声音, 如 sirens、飞机或高释放。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-125">Even if the video capture is good, the sound capture can become problematic due to off-camera sounds such as sirens, airplanes, or high winds.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-126">为了确保我们具有我们所需的所有元素, 我们使用了一系列立体声和单声道移动录音单元来获取每个位置的特定兴趣点上的异步环境元素。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-126">To assure we had all the elements we need, we used a series of stereo and mono mobile recording units to get asynchronous, ambient elements at specific points of interest in each location.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-127">此捕获非常重要, 因为它使声音设计器能够查找可在后期生产中使用的清理和可用内容, 并添加更多方向性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-127">This capture is important as it gives the sound designer the ability to seek out clean and usable content that can be used in post-production to craft interest and add further directionality.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-128">给定的捕获日期会生成大量文件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-128">Any given capture day would generate a large number of files.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-129">开发系统以跟踪与特定位置或相机拍摄相对应的文件, 这一点很重要。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-129">It was important to develop a system to track which files correspond to a particular location or camera shot.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-130">我们的记录单位设置为按日期自动命名文件并获得数字, 我们会在一天结束时将这些文件恢复到外部驱动器。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-130">Our recording unit was set up to auto-name files by date and take number and we would back these up at the end of the day to external drives.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-131">最起码, 口头方式传达 slating 的音频录制非常重要, 因为这样可以轻松地识别内容, 而文件名会成为一个问题。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-131">At the very least, verbally slating the beginning of audio recordings was important as this allows easy contextual identification of the content should filenames become a problem.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-132">当视频和音频记录为单独的媒体并且需要在 post 期间进行同步时, 我们也很重要。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-132">It was also important for us to visually slate the camera rig capture as the video and audio were recorded as separate media and needed to be synchronized during post.</span></span>

### <a name="editing-the-audio"></a><span data-ttu-id="f9237-133">编辑音频</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-133">Editing the audio</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-134">在拍摄捕获行程后的工作室, 汇编方向和沉浸式听觉体验的第一步是查看某个位置的所有音频捕获, 并确定最佳做法, 并确定在集成度.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-134">Back at the studio after the capture trip, the first step in assembling a directional and immersive aural experience is to review all of the audio capture for a location, picking out the best takes and identifying any highlights that could be applied creatively during integration.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-135">然后, 将编辑并清理音频。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-135">The audio is then edited and cleaned up.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-136">例如, 如果有一个很大的汽车, 则可以将其替换为同一个捕获中的安静环境音频部分, 并在其中拼接。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-136">For example, a loud car horn lasting a second or so and repeating a few times, can be replaced and stitched in with sections of quiet, ambient audio from the same capture.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-137">为某个位置建立视频编辑后, 声音设计器可以同步相应的音频。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-137">Once the video edit for a location has been established the sound designer can synchronize the corresponding audio.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-138">此时, 我们使用了相机远程处理捕获和移动捕获来确定哪些元素或组合可用于构建沉浸式音频场景。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-138">At this point we worked with both camera rig capture and mobile capture to decide what elements, or combination thereof, will work to build an immersive audio scene.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-139">我们发现的一项技术非常有用, 就是将所有声音元素添加到音频编辑器中, 并构建快速的线性模拟来试验各种混合创意。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-139">A technique we found useful was to add all the sound elements into an audio editor and build quick linear mock ups to experiment with different mix ideas.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-140">这为我们提供了一段时间来构建实际 HoloTour 场景提供了更好的建议。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-140">This gave us better formed ideas when it came time to build the actual HoloTour scenes.</span></span>

### <a name="assembling-the-scene"></a><span data-ttu-id="f9237-141">组合场景</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-141">Assembling the scene</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-142">构建三维环境场景的第一步是创建一个一般背景环境循环声音平台, 它将支持场景中的其他功能和交互式声音元素。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-142">The first step to building a 3D ambient scene is to create a bed of general background ambient looping sounds that will support other features and interactive sound elements in a scene.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-143">在此过程中, 我们采取了一种方法, 该方法面向由任何特定场景的特定需求和设计标准确定的不同实现技术。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-143">In doing so, we took a holistic approach towards different implementation techniques determined by the specific needs and design criteria of any particular scene.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-144">某些场景可能会使用同步的相机捕获进行索引, 而有些场景可能需要更多的特选方法, 该方法使用更单独的声音、交互式元素和音乐, 并在 HoloTour 中获得更多的迷人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-144">Some scenes might index towards using the synchronized camera capture, whereas others might require a more curated approach that uses more discretely placed sounds, interactive elements and music and sound effects for the more cinematic moments in HoloTour.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-145">使用相机捕获音频进行索引时, 我们将启用了空间声音的环境音频发射器与相机方向的方向坐标相对应, 以使 "北方相机" 视图播放来自西北麦克风的音频, 同样其他关键指导。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-145">When indexing on the use of the camera capture audio, we placed spatial sound-enabled ambient audio emitters corresponding to the directional coordinates of the camera orientation such that the north camera view plays audio from the north microphone and likewise for the other cardinal directions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-146">这些发射器是世界上锁定的, 这意味着用户可以自由地将其与这些发射器相关联, 而声音将相应变化, 从而有效地对该位置的声音建模。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-146">These emitters are world-locked, meaning the user can freely turn their head in relation to these emitters and the sound will change accordingly, effectively modeling the sound of standing at that location.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-147">倾听 Piazza Navona 或 Pantheon, 了解使用良好的相机捕获音频的场景示例。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-147">Listen to Piazza Navona or The Pantheon for examples of scenes that use a good mix of camera captured audio.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-148">一种不同的方法, 涉及到循环立体声环境与位于场景周围的空间声音发射器一起播放一种随机声音, 这些声音在量、间距和触发频率方面是随机的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-148">A different approach involved playing a looping stereo ambience in conjunction with spatial sound emitters placed around the scene playing one-off sounds that are randomized in terms of volume, pitch and trigger frequency.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-149">这将创建一个具有更强方向性的环境。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-149">This creates an ambience with an enhanced sense of directionality.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-150">例如, 在 Aguas Calientes 中, 可以倾听全景的每个象限如何具有专门突出显示地域特定区域的特定发射器, 但要一起工作以创建总体沉浸式环境。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-150">In Aguas Calientes, for example, you can listen to how each quadrant of the panorama has specific emitters that intentionally highlight specific areas of the geography, but work together to create an overall immersive ambience.</span></span>

## <a name="tips-and-tricks"></a><span data-ttu-id="f9237-151">提示和技巧</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-151">Tips and tricks</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f9237-152">当你将音频组合到一起时, 还有一些其他方法可用于进一步突出显示方向性和浸入式, 从而充分利用了 HoloLens 的空间音效功能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-152">When you're putting together audio for a scene, there are some additional methods you can use to further highlight directionality and immersion, making full use of the spatial sound capabilities of HoloLens.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-153">我们提供了以下部分的列表-在下一次尝试 HoloTour 时, 请对其进行侦听。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-153">We've provided a list of some below—listen for them the next time you try HoloTour.</span></span>

* <span data-ttu-id="f9237-161">**样条发射器**:这种特殊的空间声发射器相对于它所附加到的对象的视觉位置, 在3D 空间中移动。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-161">**Spline emitter**: This special spatial sound emitter moves in 3D space relative to the visual position of the object it's attached to.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-162">例如, 在 Machu Picchu 中, 我们使用样条发射器给出了不同的方向性和运动。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-162">An example of this was the train in Machu Picchu, where we used a spline emitter to give a distinct sense of directionality and movement.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f9237-163">**音乐和 SFX**:HoloTour 的某些方面表示更风格或迷人的方法, 它使用音乐和声音效果来提高激动人心的影响。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-163">**Music and SFX**: Certain aspects of HoloTour that represent a more stylized or cinematic approach use music and sound effects to heighten the emotional impact.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f9237-164">在罗马教程末尾的 gladiator 中, 使用 whooshes 或 stingers 等特殊效果有助于增强标签在场景中的效果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f9237-164">In the gladiator battle at the end of the Rome tour, special effects like whooshes or stingers were used to help strengthen the effect of labels appearing in scenes.</span></span>
