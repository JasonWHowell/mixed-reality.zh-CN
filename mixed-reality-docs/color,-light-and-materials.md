## <a name="rendering-on-immersive-vs-holographic-devices"></a><span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-110">在沉浸式与全息设备上渲染</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-110">Rendering on immersive vs. holographic devices</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-111">与全息耳机中呈现的内容相比，在沉浸式耳机中呈现的内容看起来会有所不同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-111">Content rendered in immersive headsets will appear visually different when compared to content rendered in holographic headsets.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-112">虽然沉浸式耳机通常会像在二维屏幕上一样呈现内容，但使用彩色的全息耳机（如 HoloLens）可以呈现全息影像。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-112">While immersive headsets generally render content much as you would expect on a 2D screen, holographic headsets like HoloLens use color-sequential, see-through RGB displays to renders holograms.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-113">在全息耳机上，请始终花时间测试您的全息体验。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-113">Always take time to test your holographic experiences in a holographic headset.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-114">即使是专门为全息设备构建内容，内容的外观也会有所不同，如辅助监视器、快照和 spectator 视图中所示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-114">The appearance of content, even if it is built specifically for holographic devices, will differ as seen on secondary monitors, snapshots, and in spectator view.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-115">请记住，在设备上进行测试，测试影像的照明，并从所有面（以及从上到下）观察内容的方式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-115">Remember to walk around experiences with a device, testing the lighting of holograms and observing from all sides (as well as from above and below) how your content is rendered.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-116">请确保在设备上的一系列亮度设置中进行测试，因为所有用户不太可能会共享假设的默认值，以及不同的照明条件集。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-116">Be sure to test at a range of brightness settings on the device, as it is unlikely all users will share an assumed default, as well as a diverse set of lighting conditions.</span></span>

## <a name="fundamentals-of-rendering-on-holographic-devices"></a><span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-117">全息设备上呈现的基本知识</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-117">Fundamentals of rendering on holographic devices</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-118">**全息设备具有累加性显示器**–通过向现实世界中的灯光添加光源来创建全息影像–白色将显示为明亮，而黑色将显示为透明。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-118">**Holographic devices have additive displays** – Holograms are created by adding light to the light from the real world – white will appear brightly, while black will appear transparent.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-119">**颜色影响因用户的环境而异**–用户房间内有很多不同的照明条件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-119">**Colors impact varies with the user’s environment** – There are many diverse lighting conditions in a user’s room.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-120">创建具有适当的对比度级别的内容，以便清晰地提供帮助。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-120">Create content with appropriate levels of contrast to help with clarity.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-121">**避免动态照明**–在全息体验中均匀发亮的全息影像是最有效的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-121">**Avoid dynamic lighting** – Holograms that are uniformly lit in holographic experiences are the most efficient.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-122">使用 "高级" 时，动态照明可能会超出移动设备的功能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-122">Using advanced, dynamic lighting will likely exceed the capabilities of mobile devices.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-123">需要动态照明时，建议使用[混合现实工具包标准着色器](https://github.com/microsoft/MixedRealityToolkit-Unity/blob/mrtk_release/Documentation/README_MRTKStandardShader.md)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-123">When dynamic lighting is required, it is recommended to use the [Mixed Reality Toolkit Standard shader](https://github.com/microsoft/MixedRealityToolkit-Unity/blob/mrtk_release/Documentation/README_MRTKStandardShader.md).</span></span> 

## <a name="designing-with-color"></a><span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-124">用 color 设计</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-124">Designing with color</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-125">由于附加显示器的性质，某些颜色在全息显示器上显示的颜色可能不同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-125">Due to the nature of additive displays, certain colors can appear different on holographic displays.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-126">某些颜色将在照明环境中弹出，而其他颜色显示的有影响力更少。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-126">Some colors will pop in lighting environments while others will appear as less impactful.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-127">冷颜色通常会 recede 到背景中，而热颜色会跳到前台。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-127">Cool colors tend to recede into the background while warm colors jump to the foreground.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-128">在浏览经验时，请考虑以下因素：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-128">Consider these factors as you explore color in your experiences:</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-129">从一种颜色的 "宽范围" 中获益，从概念上讲类似于 Adobe RGB。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-129">**Gamut** - HoloLens benefits from a "wide gamut" of color, conceptually similar to Adobe RGB.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-130">因此，某些颜色可能会在设备中表现出不同的质量和表示形式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-130">As a result, some colors can exhibit different qualities and representation in the device.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-131">**伽玛**-所呈现图像的亮度和对比度将因沉浸式和全息设备而异。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-131">**Gamma** - The brightness and contrast of the rendered image will vary between immersive and holographic devices.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-132">这些设备的差异经常会使颜色和阴影变暗，更亮或更不亮。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-132">These device differences often appear to make dark areas of color and shadows, more or less bright.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-133">**颜色分离**-也称为 "color 细分情况" 或 "color fringing"，当用户跟踪对象的眼睛时，最常发生颜色分离（包括光标）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-133">**Color separation** - Also called "color breakup" or "color fringing", color separation most commonly occurs with moving holograms (including cursor) when a user tracks objects with their eyes.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-134">**颜色一致性**-通常，全息影像呈现得非常明亮，因此，无论背景如何，都可以保持颜色一致性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-134">**Color uniformity** - Typically holograms are rendered brightly enough so that they maintain color uniformity, regardless of the background.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-135">大区域可能会变得 blotchy。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-135">Large areas may become blotchy.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-136">避免出现鲜艳、纯色的大型区域。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-136">Avoid large regions of bright, solid color.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-137">**呈现浅色颜色**-白色显示非常明亮，应慎用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-137">**Rendering light colors** - White appears very bright and should be used sparingly.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-138">大多数情况下，请考虑有关 R 235 G 235 B 235 的空白值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-138">For most cases, consider a white value around R 235 G 235 B 235.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-139">较大的亮点可能导致用户 discomfort。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-139">Large bright areas may cause user discomfort.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-140">**呈现深色颜色**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-140">**Rendering dark colors**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-141">由于加法显示的性质，深色颜色显示为透明。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-141">Due to the nature of additive displays, dark colors appear transparent.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-142">实心黑色对象将与现实环境中的显示效果完全不同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-142">A solid black object will appear no different from the real world.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-143">请参阅下面的 Alpha 通道。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-143">See Alpha channel below.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-144">若要使显示 "黑色"，请尝试使用非常深的灰色 RGB 值，如16、16、16。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-144">To give the appearance of “black” try a very dark grey RGB value such as 16,16,16.</span></span>

## <a name="technical-considerations"></a><span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-147">技术注意事项</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-147">Technical considerations</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-148">"**别名**"-深思熟虑后得出的，它是锯齿、锯齿或 "楼梯段" 的边缘与现实世界之间的边缘。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-148">**Aliasing** - Be considerate of aliasing, jagged or “stair steps” where the edge of a hologram’s geometry meets the real world.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-149">如果使用具有高细节的纹理，则会加剧此效果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-149">Using textures with high detail can aggravate this effect.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-150">应映射和筛选已启用纹理。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-150">Textures should be mapped and filtering enabled.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-151">请考虑在对象周围创建黑色边缘边框，以淡化全息影像边缘或添加纹理。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-151">Consider fading the edges of holograms or adding a texture that creates a black edge border around objects.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-152">尽可能避免精简几何。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-152">Avoid thin geometry where possible.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-153">**Alpha 通道**-对于没有渲染全息图的任何部分，必须清除 alpha 通道，使其完全透明。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-153">**Alpha channel** - You must clear your alpha channel to fully transparent for any parts where you are not rendering a hologram.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-154">在从设备或通过 Spectator 视图拍摄图像/视频时，使 alpha 不确定会导致视觉对象。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-154">Leaving the alpha undefined leads to visual artifacts when taking images/videos from the device or with Spectator View.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f6d2d-155">**纹理软化**-由于浅是全息显示屏中的加法，因此最好避免较大的纯色区域，因为它们通常不会产生预期的视觉效果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6d2d-155">**Texture softening** - Since light is additive in holographic displays, it is best to avoid large regions of bright, solid color as they often do not produce the intended visual effect.</span></span>
