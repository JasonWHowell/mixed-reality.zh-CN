## <a name="vergence-accommodation-conflict"></a><span data-ttu-id="d34eb-111">Vergence-住宿冲突</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d34eb-111">Vergence-accommodation conflict</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d34eb-117">在大多数 head 装载的显示屏中，用户将始终适应显示器的焦距距离（以获取清晰的图像），但会收敛到对象的距离（以获取单个图像）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d34eb-117">In most head-mounted displays users will always accommodate to the focal distance of the display (to get a sharp image), but converge to the distance of the object of interest (to get a single image).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d34eb-118">当用户容纳并聚合到不同的距离时，两个提示之间的自然链接必须中断，这可能会导致 visual discomfort 或疲劳。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d34eb-118">When users accommodate and converge to different distances, the natural link between the two cues must be broken and this can lead to visual discomfort or fatigue.</span></span>
