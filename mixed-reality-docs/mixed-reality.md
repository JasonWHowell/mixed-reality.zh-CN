# <a name="what-is-mixed-reality"></a><span data-ttu-id="3687a-104">什么被混合现实？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-104">What is mixed reality?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3687a-105">混合的现实是混合现实世界与数字世界的结果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-105">Mixed reality is the result of blending the physical world with the digital world.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-106">混合的现实是用户、 计算机和环境交互的下一个演化，解锁之前现在已限制为我们想象力的可能性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-106">Mixed reality is the next evolution in human, computer, and environment interaction and unlocks possibilities that before now were restricted to our imaginations.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-107">它是通过实现计算机视觉、 图形处理能力、 显示技术和输入的系统中的高级功能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-107">It is made possible by advancements in computer vision, graphical processing power, display technology, and input systems.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-108">术语*混合现实*最初由 Paul Milgram 和 Fumio Kishino 引入 1994年白皮书中"[分类的混合现实的视觉显示](http://etclab.mie.utoronto.ca/people/paul_dir/IEICE94/ieice.html)。"</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-108">The term *mixed reality* was originally introduced in a 1994 paper by Paul Milgram and Fumio Kishino, "[A Taxonomy of Mixed Reality Visual Displays](http://etclab.mie.utoronto.ca/people/paul_dir/IEICE94/ieice.html)."</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-109">引入了的概念，其纸张*virtuality continuum* ，专注于应用于分类的分类的显示方式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-109">Their paper introduced the concept of the *virtuality continuum* and focused on how the categorization of taxonomy applied to displays.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-110">从那时起，混合现实的应用程序将显示更高版本，但还包括环境的输入、 空间声音和位置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-110">Since then, the application of mixed reality goes beyond displays but also includes environmental input, spatial sound, and location.</span></span>

## <a name="environmental-input-and-perception"></a><span data-ttu-id="3687a-111">环境的输入和感知</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-111">Environmental input and perception</span></span>

![显示计算机、 人和环境之间的交互的维恩图](images/mixed-reality-venn-diagram-300px.png)<br> 

<span data-ttu-id="3687a-113">在过去的几个几十年来，通过人工输入与计算机输入之间的关系已还探讨了。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-113">Over the past several decades, the relationship between human input and computer input has been well explored.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-114">它甚至还包含名为广为研究的专业*人机交互*或 HCI。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-114">It even has a widely studied discipline known as *human computer interaction* or HCI.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-115">通过各种方法包括键盘、 鼠标、 触控、 手写内容、 语音、 和甚至 Kinect 主干跟踪发生人工输入。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-115">Human input happens through a variety of means including keyboards, mice, touch, ink, voice, and even Kinect skeletal tracking.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3687a-116">中的传感器和处理的高级功能即表示授予上升到的计算机输入新的区域从环境。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-116">Advancements in sensors and processing are giving rise to a new area of computer input from environments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-117">计算机和环境之间的交互是有效环境的了解，或*perception*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-117">The interaction between computers and environments is effectively environmental understanding, or *perception*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-118">因此调用显示环境信息的 Windows 中的 API 名称[perception Api](https://docs.microsoft.com/uwp/api/Windows.Perception)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-118">Hence the API names in Windows that reveal environmental information are called the [perception APIs](https://docs.microsoft.com/uwp/api/Windows.Perception).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-119">环境输入捕获等人的世界中的位置 (例如[头跟踪](coordinate-systems.md))，图面和边界 (例如[空间映射](spatial-mapping.md)和[空间了解](case-study-expanding-the-spatial-mapping-capabilities-of-hololens.md))，环境照明、 环境声音、 对象识别和位置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-119">Environmental input captures things like a person's position in the world (e.g. [head tracking](coordinate-systems.md)), surfaces and boundaries (e.g. [spatial mapping](spatial-mapping.md) and [spatial understanding](case-study-expanding-the-spatial-mapping-capabilities-of-hololens.md)), ambient lighting, environmental sound, object recognition, and location.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3687a-120">现在，所有三个 – 计算机的处理相结合，人工输入和环境输入 – 设置来创建，则返回 true 的混合的现实体验的机会。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-120">Now, the combination of all three – computer processing, human input, and environmental input – sets the opportunity to create true mixed reality experiences.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-121">通过物理世界移动可以将转换为数字世界中的移动。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-121">Movement through the physical world can translate to movement in the digital world.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-122">现实生活中的边界可能会影响应用程序的体验，例如玩游戏，在数字世界中。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-122">Boundaries in the physical world can influence application experiences, such as game play, in the digital world.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-123">而无需环境输入体验不能混合之间物理和数字的实际问题。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-123">Without environmental input, experiences cannot blend between the physical and digital realities.</span></span>

## <a name="the-mixed-reality-spectrum"></a><span data-ttu-id="3687a-124">混合的现实范围</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-124">The mixed reality spectrum</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3687a-125">由于混合的现实是数字世界的现实世界的混合，这些两个现实情况定义名为 virtuality continuum 提供了一系列的极坐标图的端。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-125">Since mixed reality is the blending of the physical world and digital world, these two realities define the polar ends of a spectrum known as the virtuality continuum.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-126">为简单起见，我们将它称为*混合的现实范围*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-126">For simplicity, we refer to this as the *mixed reality spectrum*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-127">在左侧，我们在其中我们人类，存在物理现实。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-127">On the left-hand side we have physical reality in which we, humans, exist.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-128">然后在右侧上有相应的数字现实。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-128">Then on the right-hand side we have the corresponding digital reality.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3687a-129">市场上大多数移动电话现在有一些不环境了解功能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-129">Most mobile phones on the market today have little to no environmental understanding capabilities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-130">因此，它们提供的体验不能混合之间物理和数字的实际问题。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-130">Thus the experiences they offer cannot mix between physical and digital realities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-131">覆盖层上的物理世界的视频流的图形体验*增强现实*，并体验，遮蔽视图，以便提供一种数字体验*虚拟现实*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-131">The experiences that overlay graphics on video streams of the physical world are *augmented reality*, and the experiences that occlude your view to present a digital experience are *virtual reality*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-132">正如您所看到的是这两个极端之间启用了体验*混合现实*:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-132">As you can see, the experiences enabled between these two extremes is *mixed reality*:</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="3687a-133">启动与物理世界，放置一个数字的对象，例如一张全息图，就像它是确实存在。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-133">Starting with the physical world, placing a digital object, such as a hologram, as if it was really there.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="3687a-134">– 头像 – 从真实的场景中，另一个人的数字表示开始显示，它们站着离开说明时的位置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-134">Starting with the physical world, a digital representation of another person – an avatar – shows the location where they were standing when leaving notes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-135">换而言之，表示异步协作的不同时间点在时间中的体验。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-135">In other words, experiences that represent asynchronous collaboration at different points in time.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="3687a-136">从开始的数字世界，物理边界从真实的场景中，如墙壁和家具，数字中显示了体验，以帮助用户避免物理对象。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-136">Starting with a digital world, physical boundaries from the physical world, such as walls and furniture, appear digitally within the experience to help users avoid physical objects.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3687a-138">最增强的现实和虚拟现实产品/服务提供立即表示此频谱的一小部分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-138">Most augmented reality and virtual reality offerings available today represent a very small part of this spectrum.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-139">它们，但是，在更大的混合的现实范畴内形成的子集。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-139">They are, however, subsets of the larger mixed reality spectrum.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-140">Windows 10 的一点是，所有使用构建而成，并允许混合的人物、 地点和操作与现实世界的数字表示形式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-140">Windows 10 is built with the entire spectrum in mind, and allows blending digital representations of people, places and things with the real world.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3687a-142">有两种主要类型的设备提供的 Windows Mixed Reality 体验：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-142">There are two main types of devices that deliver Windows Mixed Reality experiences:</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="3687a-143">**Holographic 的设备。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-143">**Holographic devices.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3687a-144">这些的特征是设备的能力，就好像确实可以将数字内容放置在现实生活中。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-144">These are characterized by the device's ability to place digital content in the real world as if it were really there.</span></span>

## <a name="devices-and-experiences"></a><span data-ttu-id="3687a-163">设备和体验</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3687a-163">Devices and experiences</span></span>
