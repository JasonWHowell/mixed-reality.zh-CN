<span data-ttu-id="30ac9-175">这是有点棘手。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-175">This is a little more tricky.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-176">在遇到 holographic"旋转"按钮以点击有利于可怕的体验。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-176">Having holographic “rotate” buttons to tap makes for a terrible experience.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-177">不存在的物理控件为充分利用，因此行为必须来自操作的一个对象，表示 lander，或使用 lander 本身。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-177">There isn’t a physical control to leverage, so the behavior must come from manipulation of an object representing the lander, or with the lander itself.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-178">我们想出了使用点击并拖动到有效地"推送和拉取"用户可以使用它的方法在方向他们希望面临着。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-178">We came up with a method using tap-and-drag which enables a user to effectively “push and pull” it in the direction they want it to face.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-179">任何时候用户点击并按住，初始化手势的位置的空间中的点变得旋转的原点。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-179">Any time a user taps and holds, the point in space where the gesture was initiated becomes the origin for rotation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-180">拖动源将转换现有的转换 （X，Y，Z） 的增量，并将其应用到 lander 的旋转值的增量。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-180">Dragging from the origin converts the delta of the hand's translation (X,Y,Z) and applies it to the delta of the lander's rotation values.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-181">或再简单*相应地返回在空间中拖动左 <>-右、 向上 <>-向下转发 <>-旋转飞船*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-181">Or more simply, *dragging left <-> right, up <-> down, forward <-> back in spaces rotates the ship accordingly*.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="30ac9-182">由于 HoloLens 可以跟踪两只手，旋转可以分配到右侧而主旨是由左侧控制。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-182">Since the HoloLens can track two hands, rotation can be assigned to the right hand while thrust is controlled by the left.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-183">注意技巧是在此游戏中成功的驱动因素。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-183">Finesse is the driving factor for success in this game.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-184">*感觉*这些交互是绝对的最高优先级。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-184">The *feel* of these interactions is the absolute highest priority.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-185">尤其是在上下文中的边用浸入式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-185">Especially in context of tactile immersion.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-186">过快地做出反应的发货很难不必要地来进行控制，而一个太慢会要求用户在"推送和拉取"飞船上滑雪长一段时间。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-186">A ship that reacts too quickly would be unnecessarily difficult to steer, while one too slow would require the user to “push and pull” on the ship for an awkwardly long amount of time.</span></span>

### <a name="adding-input-for-game-controllers"></a><span data-ttu-id="30ac9-187">正在添加输入游戏控制器</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-187">Adding input for game controllers</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="30ac9-188">HoloLens 上的手势提供细粒度控制新颖方法，而是仍缺少 'true' 获取从模拟控件的边用反馈。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-188">While hand gestures on the HoloLens provide a novel method of fine-grain control, there is still a certain lack of 'true' tactile feedback that you get from analog controls.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-189">连接 Xbox 游戏控制器允许将带回 physicality 这种情况下同时利用将附着要保留的细粒度控制。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-189">Connecting an Xbox game controller allows to bring back this sense of physicality while leveraging the control sticks to retain fine-grain control.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="30ac9-190">有多种方法来将比较直接的控制方案应用到 Xbox 控制器。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-190">There are multiple ways to apply the relatively straight-forward control scheme to the Xbox controller.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-191">由于我们要保持原始拱廊设置尽可能接近**被迫接受了**将最佳映射到触发器按钮。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-191">Since we're trying to stay as close to the original arcade set up as possible, **Thrust** maps best to the trigger button.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-192">这些按钮是模拟的控件，这意味着它们具有多个简单*打开和关闭*指出，他们可以实际响应将放入其压力的程度。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-192">These buttons are analog controls, meaning they have more than simple *on and off* states, they actually respond to the degree of pressure put on them.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-193">这为我们提供了类似构造作为**主旨是杆**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-193">This gives us a similar construct as the **thrust lever**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-194">不同于原始游戏和手笔势，此控件将剪切飞船的主旨是后用户停止触发器带来压力。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-194">Unlike the original game and the hand gesture, this control will cut the ship's thrust once a user stops putting pressure on the trigger.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-195">它仍提供用户同等程度的注意技巧原始拱廊游戏一样。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-195">It still gives the user the same degree of finesse as the original arcade game did.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="30ac9-196">![左的摇杆进行映射，以偏航角和回滚、 右摇杆进行映射，以宣传和回滚](images/thumbsticksidebyside.gif)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-196">![Left thumbstick is mapped to Yaw and Roll, Right thumbstick is mapped to Pitch and Roll](images/thumbsticksidebyside.gif)</span></span><br>
<span data-ttu-id="30ac9-197">*左的摇杆进行映射，以偏航角和回滚;右摇杆进行映射，以宣传和回滚*</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-197">*Left thumbstick is mapped to yaw and roll; right thumbstick is mapped to pitch and roll*</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="30ac9-198">双 thumbsticks 自然地适用于控制的飞船旋转。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-198">The dual thumbsticks naturally lend themselves to controlling ship rotation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-199">遗憾的是，有 3 个轴上的发货可以旋转和两个 thumbsticks 这两者都支持两个轴。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-199">Unfortunately, there are 3 axes on which the ship can rotate and two thumbsticks which both support two axes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-200">这种不匹配意味着任一一个摇杆控件一个轴;或重叠的 thumbsticks 的轴。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-200">This mismatch means either one thumbstick controls one axis; or there is overlap of axes for the thumbsticks.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-201">前一种解决方案最终不会感觉"中断"，因为 thumbsticks 本质上是混合使用其本地 X 和 Y 值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-201">The former solution ended up feeling "broken" since thumbsticks inherently blend their local X and Y values.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-202">后一种解决方案时，需要查找的冗余轴感觉最自然一些测试。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-202">The latter solution required some testing to find which redundant axes feel the most natural.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-203">最后一个示例使用*偏航角*和*回滚*（Y 和 X 轴） 的左摇杆和*间距*并*回滚*（Z 和 X 轴） 的权利摇杆。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-203">The final sample uses *yaw* and *roll* (Y and X axes) for the left thumbstick, and *pitch* and *roll* (Z and X axes) for the right thumbstick.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-204">这感觉最自然作为*回滚*似乎也与独立配对*偏航角*并*间距*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-204">This felt the most natural as *roll* seems to independently pair well with *yaw* and *pitch*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-205">请注意，使用为这两个 thumbsticks*回滚*也恰好双击旋转值中; 它是非常有趣了具有 lander do 循环。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-205">As a side note, using both thumbsticks for *roll* also happens to double the rotation value; it's pretty fun to have the lander do loops.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="30ac9-206">此示例应用演示了如何空间识别和触觉浸入式都明显改变了由于 Windows Mixed Reality 可扩展输入形式的体验。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-206">This sample app demonstrates how spatial recognition and tactile immersion can significantly change an experience thanks to Windows Mixed Reality's extensible input modalities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-207">尽管但阴历 Lander 可能接近年龄在 40 年，概念公开与小八边形与 legs 将 live 永远持续下去。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-207">While Lunar Lander may be nearing 40 years in age, the concepts exposed with that little octagon-with-legs will live on forever.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30ac9-208">当设想未来，为什么不查看过去？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30ac9-208">When imagining the future, why not look at the past?</span></span>
